Our Story

The Cozy Cup began as a neighborhood gathering place, built on a love for ethically sourced beans, home-baked pastries, and meaningful conversations. We partner with small farms and roasters who share our commitment to quality and community.

Our Values

Sourcing with Integrity

We seek beans with transparent origins and fair compensation for farmers.

Community First

We host local events, meetups, and study nights to foster connection.

Quality Craft

From bean to cup, we obsess over the little details that elevate flavor.
